Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we collect, use and protect your information.

Effective Date: January 15, 2025

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. It is Blankonia's policy to respect your privacy and comply with any applicable law and regulation regarding any personal information we may collect about you, including across our website, blankonia.com, and other sites we own and operate.

This policy is effective as of January 15, 2025 and was last updated on January 15, 2025.

Information We Collect

Information we collect includes both information you knowingly and actively provide us when using or participating in any of our services and promotions, and any information automatically sent by your devices in the course of accessing our products and services.

Personal Information

We may ask for personal information which may include one or more of the following:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Date of birth
  • Booking preferences
  • Payment information (processed securely)

Log Data

When you visit our website, our servers may automatically log the standard data provided by your web browser. It may include your device's Internet Protocol (IP) address, your browser type and version, the pages you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on each page, and other details about your visit.

How We Use Information

We may collect, hold, use, and disclose information for the following purposes:

  • To provide you with our accommodation services
  • To process bookings and payments
  • To contact and communicate with you
  • To improve our website and services
  • For security and fraud prevention
  • To comply with our legal obligations

Cookies

We use "cookies" to collect information about you and your activity across our site. A cookie is a small piece of data that our website stores on your computer, and accesses each time you visit, so we can understand how you use our site. This helps us serve you content based on preferences you have specified.

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed.

Data Security

When we collect and process personal information, and while we retain this information, we will protect it within commercially acceptable means to prevent loss and theft, as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use, or modification.

Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You can request details of the personal information we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate information
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a structured format
  • Right to Object: You can object to certain types of processing
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw your consent at any time

International Transfers

The personal information we collect is stored and/or processed in Greece and may be transferred to countries outside the European Economic Area. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

Changes to This Policy

At our discretion, we may change our privacy policy to reflect updates to our business processes, current acceptable practices, or legislative or regulatory changes. If we decide to change this privacy policy, we will post the changes here at the same link by which you are accessing this privacy policy.
